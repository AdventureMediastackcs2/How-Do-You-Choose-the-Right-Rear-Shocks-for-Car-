Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can a driver confirm that this rear shock package fits their car?
A: This package is designed to fit Toyota 4Runner models from 2010 to 2024, fifth Generation. Always confirm your vehicle's year before purchasing.

Q: Will any additional parts be needed after installing this kit?
A: The kit is fully bolt-on for the rear shocks and front coilovers. You may optionally consider an alignment or adjustable upper control arms when adding heavier front accessories for optimal performance.

Q: How often should the shocks be serviced or inspected?
A: Regular inspection is recommended every 50,000 kilometers or sooner if you frequently drive off-road. Routine checks for seals and leaks help maintain long-term performance.

Q: Can the kit be installed by the vehicle owner?
A: Installation is possible for drivers with mechanical experience and proper tools. Professional installation is recommended to ensure correct torque and alignment for the best results.

Q: Will my ride height or handling change?
A: The front lift is approximately two inches with matched rear damping, providing a mild lift. Drivers will enjoy firmer handling than stock with more controlled body motion and improved stability on rough surfaces.
